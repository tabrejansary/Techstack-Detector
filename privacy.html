Privacy Policy

At TechStack Detector, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any information you provide when using our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

TechStack Detector does not collect personally identifiable information. All analysis is performed locally in your browser, and no data is sent to our servers.

Permissions

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to detect technologies on the active website you visit.
  • scripting: Needed to run analysis scripts on the page.
  • storage: Used to save user preferences locally.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any data to third parties.

Cookies

This extension does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

Security

All data is processed locally. We do not transmit or store your browsing data on any server, ensuring your information remains private and secure.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Please check back here to stay informed about our privacy practices.
